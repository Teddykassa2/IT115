The Legeng of Zelda[a] is a fantasy action-adventure video game franchise created by Japanese game designer Shigeru Miyamoto and Takashi Tezuka. It is primarily developed and published by Nintendo, although some portable installments and re-release have been outsourced to Capcom, Vanpool, and Gezzo. The gameplay incorporates action-adventure and element of action RPG games.
